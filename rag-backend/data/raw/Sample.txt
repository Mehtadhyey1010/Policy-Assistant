1. INTRODUCTION
=====================================================================

This Policy Rulebook establishes the framework governing employee 
conduct, responsibilities, rights, and organizational expectations. 
All employees, contractors, interns, consultants, and temporary staff 
must comply with the policies outlined herein.

Failure to adhere to these policies may result in disciplinary action, 
including termination.

=====================================================================
2. CODE OF CONDUCT
=====================================================================

2.1 Professional Behavior
Employees must conduct themselves professionally and respectfully at 
all times.

2.2 Integrity
Employees shall not engage in bribery, fraud, corruption, or unethical 
practices.

2.3 Conflict of Interest
Employees must disclose any financial, personal, or external business 
interests that conflict with company objectives.

2.4 Gifts & Hospitality
Employees may not accept gifts exceeding $100 in value without written 
approval from senior management.

=====================================================================
3. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
=====================================================================

3.1 Employment Categories
- Full-Time
- Part-Time
- Contractual
- Intern
- Probationary

3.2 Probation Period
All new hires are subject to a probation period of 6 months.

3.3 Confirmation of Employment
Upon successful completion of probation, written confirmation shall be 
provided.

=====================================================================
4. WORK HOURS AND ATTENDANCE
=====================================================================

4.1 Standard Work Hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

4.2 Overtime
Overtime must be pre-approved. Compensation follows labor law standards.

4.3 Late Attendance
More than 3 late arrivals in a month may result in a warning.

4.4 Absenteeism
Uninformed absence beyond 2 consecutive days may lead to disciplinary 
review.

=====================================================================
5. LEAVE POLICY
=====================================================================

5.1 Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 21 paid annual leave days per year.

5.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 paid sick leave days annually.

5.3 Casual Leave
Employees may avail 8 casual leave days per year.

5.4 Maternity Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

5.5 Paternity Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 10 working days of paid paternity 
leave.

5.6 Leave Approval
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 3 days 
in advance (except emergencies).

=====================================================================
6. COMPENSATION & PAYROLL
=====================================================================

6.1 Salary Payment
Salary is credited on the last working day of each month.

6.2 Deductions
Applicable taxes and statutory deductions shall apply.

6.3 Bonuses
Performance bonuses are discretionary and based on annual reviews.

=====================================================================
7. REMOTE WORK POLICY
=====================================================================

7.1 Eligibility
Employees may request remote work subject to managerial approval.

7.2 Remote Work Days
Maximum 3 days per week permitted.

7.3 Equipment
Employees are responsible for maintaining company-issued devices.

7.4 Data Security
Remote access must use VPN and two-factor authentication.

=====================================================================
8. IT & DATA SECURITY POLICY
=====================================================================

8.1 Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include symbols.

8.2 Data Classification
Data is categorized as:
- Public
- Internal
- Confidential
- Restricted

8.3 Access Control
Access to restricted systems requires managerial authorization.

8.4 Data Breach Reporting
All suspected breaches must be reported within 24 hours.

=====================================================================
9. CONFIDENTIALITY & NDA
=====================================================================

Employees must not disclose trade secrets, client information, 
intellectual property, or proprietary systems.

Violation may result in legal action.

=====================================================================
10. ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY
=====================================================================

The organization maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment.

Harassment includes:
- Verbal abuse
- Physical intimidation
- Sexual harassment
- Cyber harassment

Complaints may be filed anonymously through the Ethics Committee.

=====================================================================
11. EQUAL OPPORTUNITY POLICY
=====================================================================

The organization prohibits discrimination based on:
- Gender
- Religion
- Race
- Disability
- Nationality
- Age

=====================================================================
12. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
=====================================================================

12.1 Review Cycle
Annual reviews occur every December.

12.2 KPIs
Employees are evaluated based on measurable KPIs.

12.3 Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
Underperforming employees may be placed on a 60-day PIP.

=====================================================================
13. TRAVEL & REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
=====================================================================

13.1 Travel Approval
All business travel must be pre-approved.

13.2 Air Travel
Economy class for employees below Director level.

13.3 Hotel Stay
Maximum reimbursement: $150 per night.

13.4 Meal Allowance
Daily meal allowance capped at $40.

13.5 Expense Submission
Expenses must be submitted within 7 working days of travel completion.

=====================================================================
14. HEALTH & SAFETY POLICY
=====================================================================

Employees must follow all workplace safety standards.

Emergency exits must remain unobstructed.

Safety drills shall be conducted twice per year.

=====================================================================
15. DISCIPLINARY PROCEDURES
=====================================================================

15.1 Verbal Warning
15.2 Written Warning
15.3 Final Warning
15.4 Suspension
15.5 Termination

Serious misconduct may bypass intermediate steps.

=====================================================================
16. TERMINATION POLICY
=====================================================================

16.1 Resignation Notice
Minimum 30 days written notice required.

16.2 Immediate Termination
May occur in cases of fraud, violence, or severe misconduct.

16.3 Final Settlement
Settlement processed within 30 days of last working day.

=====================================================================
17. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
=====================================================================

Employees may escalate complaints to:
- Immediate Manager
- HR Department
- Ethics Committee

All grievances shall be investigated within 15 working days.

=====================================================================
18. ETHICS & COMPLIANCE
=====================================================================

Employees must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Whistleblower protection is guaranteed.

=====================================================================
19. SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY
=====================================================================

Employees must not post confidential company information online.

Defamatory content against the organization is prohibited.

=====================================================================
20. DOCUMENT CONTROL & AMENDMENTS
=====================================================================

The organization reserves the right to amend policies at any time.

All updates will be communicated via official email.
